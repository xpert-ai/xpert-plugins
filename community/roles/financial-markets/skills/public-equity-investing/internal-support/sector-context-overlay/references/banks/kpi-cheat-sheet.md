# KPI Cheat Sheet

Deferred reference for `Bank Sector Analysis`. Load this only when the task needs this level of sector detail.

## Bank KPI Map

## 1) Franchise and funding quality
### Metrics to track
- deposit growth by type
- noninterest-bearing deposit mix
- loan-to-deposit ratio
- cumulative and incremental deposit beta
- cost of deposits and cost of interest-bearing liabilities
- uninsured deposit share
- wholesale funding share
- average versus period-end deposits

## 2) Asset mix, repricing, and net interest income
### Metrics to track
- NII and NIM
- ex-markets or adjusted NII where relevant
- asset sensitivity / liability sensitivity tables
- earning-asset mix
- deposit beta and remix
- securities yield versus duration
- loan yield, deposit cost, funding cost, and spread by major segment

## 3) Credit quality and reserve adequacy
### Metrics to track
- nonperforming assets / loans
- nonaccruals
- criticized / classified assets if disclosed
- 30+/60+/90+ delinquencies
- net charge-offs and annualized NCO rate
- provision expense
- ACL / loans and ACL / NCOs
- coverage of nonaccruals and NPLs
- modified / troubled loans, deferrals, or other watchlist indicators

## 4) Securities book, AOCI, and hidden balance-sheet pressure
### Metrics to track
- AFS and HTM securities balances
- unrealized gains/losses by book
- AOCI impact on tangible book and regulatory capital
- duration, yield, and repricing profile where disclosed
- cash-flow hedge marks and hedge effectiveness where relevant
- tangible common equity and capital ratios with and without AOCI
- securities losses required to reposition the balance sheet

## 5) Capital as both constraint and catalyst
### Metrics to track
- CET1 ratio and CET1 dollars
- standardized and advanced CET1 where relevant
- leverage ratio / SLR
- total capital ratio
- stress capital buffer or local equivalent
- RWA growth and RWA density
- TCE, tangible book value per share, and ROTCE
- payout ratio, dividend capacity, and buyback capacity

## 6) Liquidity, contingency funding, and confidence risk
### Metrics to track
- HQLA / liquid assets if disclosed
- LCR and NSFR where relevant
- cash and reserve balances
- available borrowing capacity
- encumbered versus unencumbered assets
- uninsured and concentrated deposits
- liquidity coverage of uninsured deposits or short-term liabilities

## 7) Fees, expenses, and operating leverage
### Metrics to track
- noninterest income by recurring versus market-sensitive fee pool
- wealth, custody, card, payments, mortgage, IB, markets, and servicing fee drivers where relevant
- efficiency ratio and adjusted expense ratio
- compensation, technology, branch, regulatory remediation, FDIC/special assessments, litigation, and restructuring costs
- operating leverage excluding volatile fee income and one-time items

## 8) Public-equity security context and Credit Markets handoff
### Metrics to track
- normalized EPS, ROTCE, tangible book value per share, and P/TBV for equity
- preferred/AT1/T2, holdco/opco debt, rating triggers, maturities, and recovery path only as Credit Markets handoff fields
- instrument-level yield/spread, price, call economics, and downside recovery assumptions route to Credit Markets; retain only common-equity confidence, capital, and funding read-through
