# Sector PM Judgment Heuristics

Use this reference to adapt sector context for public-equity PM decision support. Sector context should sharpen the investment decision, not become a generic sector primer.

## Taste Rules

- Identify the sector-specific PM debate before listing KPIs.
- Name the KPI hierarchy: primary driver, secondary driver, and common false signal.
- State what is priced in and which sector convention the market is likely using.
- Distinguish durable signal from one-quarter noise.
- Identify valuation convention and the metric that would force multiple expansion or compression.
- Flag source gaps and unsupported precision.
- Force falsifiers: what evidence would break the sector thesis or change sizing?
