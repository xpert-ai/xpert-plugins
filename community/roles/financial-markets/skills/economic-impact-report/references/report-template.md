For standalone HTML reports, keep the first-read layer compact: `Executive Call`, `Event Status And Market Baseline`, the transmission map, and ranked equity impact map should be visible before detailed scenario, monitoring, and source-ledger material. Cite load-bearing facts near use, but prefer a short section-level source note over repeated source chips in every adjacent module.
